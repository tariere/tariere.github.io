Max Temperature Analysis

In looking at latitude vs max temperature, we can see that the weather gets warmer as the equator (0 Degrees latitude) is approached. Another observation is that the southern hemisphere appears to have more warm tempuratures than the northern hemisphere for the time of the year (January) and this likely due to the earth's tilt in January.
